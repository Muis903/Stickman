Stickman is a pretty famous game in the world!
The meaning is to guess the word the way to choosing the letter,
and than you get to see if the chosen letter in thast word is.

How it works:
1. Computer invents an random word and says how mutch letters it has.
2. You, as player, get 5 lives to play and try to guess the word per letter.
3. If chosen letter is in the word, you save your lives and play on.
4. If chosen letter is not in the word, you lose 1 live. 
5. If you have no lives, the game stops.
